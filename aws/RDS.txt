Database instances can be stored with:
  General Purpose SSD:
  It is actually for cost-effective, moderate I/O workloads
  - 3,000 IOPS till 16,000 IOPS.
  It has gp2, gp3 with gp3 has more of the processing power.
  Ideal for small to medium sized databases for development and test environments.

  Provisioned IOPS SSD:
  It is for High performance I/O intensive workloads
  - 64,000 IOPS till 256,000 IOPS.
  But it also guarantees performance.
  Ideal for mission-critical apps and also for Prod environments.
